Crispy Chilioil

Hello my dear friends! You found the way to the spicy part of the page. Welcome to our verys easy and delicious Crispy Chilioil! With little effort and big outcome you will create a wonderful spicy seasoning, which can be used for every purpose, be it fried egg, chicken wings, chicken nuggets or plain on rice for our simple visitors.

    Ingredients

  • x7-10 Garliccloves
  • x3 Shallots
  • x10-12 Peppercorns
  • 1 Tablespoon Brown Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Smoked Paprica
  • 150gr Chiliflakes
  • 2 Tablespoons Soysauce
  • 350ml Vegtable Oil (Sunflower)

Instruction

For your Crispy Chilioil, you have to cut your shallots into thin slices. the same goes for your garlic. Put everything into a metalbowl or some container which can take some heat. crush your peppercorns or use some grounded pepper if prefered and those to your bow. Then add your brown sugar, smoked paprica, chiliflakes and your soysauce, then mix everything well. Next up heat your vegtable oil to roughly 170Â°C, for this it's nice to use a thermometer. Once your oil reaches the required temperature, pour it all over your chilimixture. BE CAREFUL WITH THE OIL!! As you can see in the Video the mixture will bubble extremly and the steam coming up is also very hot, so please be careful. Once you poured everything on to your delicous mixture, let it cool down and transfer it into a nice sealable jar. And there it is, your crispy chilioil, enjoy it and tell your friends about it! :)
